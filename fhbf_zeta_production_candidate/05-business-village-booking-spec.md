# Business Village Booking Specification

## Stand Types and Pricing

| Stand Type | Member | Non-member |
|---|---:|---:|
| Emerging Entrepreneur | 30 | 60 |
| Standard Indoor | 100 | 150 |
| Premium Indoor | 150 | 250 |
| Outdoor Display | 150 | 250 |
| Vehicle Display | 200 | 350 |
| Equipment Display | 250 | 400 |

## Stand Statuses
- AVAILABLE
- RESERVED
- BOOKED
- BLOCKED

## Booking Rules
- Member price requires valid active membership number
- Stand is reserved after application
- Stand becomes booked only after payment confirmation
- Admin can override allocation
- Vehicle displays require admin approval
