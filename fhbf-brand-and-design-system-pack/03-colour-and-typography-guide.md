# FHBF Colour & Typography Guide

## Primary Colours
- Deep Purple: #1E003C
- Royal Purple: #2B004D
- Mid Purple: #3D0066
- Kingdom Gold: #D4AF37
- White: #FFFFFF
- Soft Lilac: #F8F5FF
- Charcoal: #222222

## Suggested Gradients
- #1E003C to #3D0066
- #D4AF37 to #F1D36B

## Typography
Headings: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, Merriweather  
Body: Inter, Poppins, DM Sans, Open Sans
