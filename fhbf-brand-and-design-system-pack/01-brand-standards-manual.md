# FHBF Brand Standards Manual

## Brand Name
Faith Heights Business Fellowship

## Short Name
FHBF

## Brand Position
A Kingdom Enterprise Ecosystem.

## Brand Statement
Faith Heights Business Fellowship is a Kingdom business ecosystem connecting entrepreneurs, professionals, investors, organisations and communities through enterprise, opportunity, innovation and impact.

## Core Taglines
- More Networking. More Growth. More Opportunities.
- Building Businesses. Developing Leaders. Transforming Nations.
- Influencing the Seven Mountains Through Kingdom Enterprise.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Kingdom-minded, trusted, innovative, aspirational, welcoming, excellent and ethical.

## Tone of Voice
Warm but professional, faith-centred but business-focused, clear, confident and practical.

## Language To Avoid
Political slogans, party language, aggressive sales language, promises of guaranteed profit and unverified claims.
