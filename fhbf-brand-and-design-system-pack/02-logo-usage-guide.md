# FHBF Logo Usage Guide

## Logo Versions
- Primary full colour logo
- Gold logo for dark backgrounds
- White logo for dark/busy backgrounds
- Icon/favicon for app icon, browser tab and small spaces

## Clear Space
Leave space around the logo equal to at least the height of the mountain icon.

## Do Not
- Stretch the logo
- Change the mountain proportions
- Place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Use colours outside the approved palette
- Crop the logo incorrectly

Use the icon on the left with text: Faith Heights Business Fellowship.
