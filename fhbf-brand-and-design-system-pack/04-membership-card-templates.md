# FHBF Membership Card Templates

## Card Information
- FHBF logo
- Member photo
- Member name
- Membership number
- Membership level
- Business name
- Chapter/country
- Expiry date
- QR code

## Membership Number Format
FHBF-YYYY-000001

## Card Levels
- Student Member
- Entrepreneur Member
- Professional Member
- Business Member
- Corporate Partner
- Founding Member

## Card Text
Faith Heights Business Fellowship  
Digital Membership Card

Name: {Member Name}  
Level: {Membership Level}  
Member No: {Membership Number}  
Valid Until: {Expiry Date}
