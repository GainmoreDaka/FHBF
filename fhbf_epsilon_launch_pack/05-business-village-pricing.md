Non-members: US$5 per day or US$15 week pass

## Sponsor Packages

Bronze Sponsor: US$500+

Gold Sponsor: US$2,500+  
Platinum Partner: US$5,000+  

## Pricing Logic

When someone books a stand, the system asks:

Are you an FHBF member?

If yes:
- Enter membership number
- System applies member price

If no:
- System applies non-member price
- Website suggests joining FHBF first to save money
