# FHBF QR Ecosystem

## QR Types

### Membership QR
Used for:
- Member verification
- Event access
- Member discounts
- Business Village entry

### Event QR
Used for:
- Registration confirmation
- Check-in
- Attendance reports

### Business Village QR
Used for:
- Exhibitor stand verification
- Visitor navigation
- Business profile access

### Directory QR
Placed on banners/cards:
- Opens business profile
- Allows visitor to contact business

### Trade Mission QR
Used for:
- Participant verification
- Travel document tracking
- Mission attendance
