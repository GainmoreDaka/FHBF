# FHBF Epsilon Launch Pack

Faith Heights Business Fellowship (FHBF) Epsilon is the launch planning and production-design pack for turning FHBF into a year-round Kingdom Business Ecosystem.

This pack builds on the Alpha, Beta, Gamma and Delta work and focuses on production readiness, launch planning, database expansion, UI design, mobile app planning, QR workflows, WhatsApp flows and onboarding strategy.

## Contents

- Production roadmap
- Expanded database model
- UI design system
- Membership launch plan
- Business Village pricing
- Website content pack
- WhatsApp notification architecture
- QR ecosystem
- Mobile app specification
- Admin workflows
- Launch checklist
