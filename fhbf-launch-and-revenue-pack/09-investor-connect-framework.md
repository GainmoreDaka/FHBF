# FHBF Investor Connect Framework

## Purpose
To connect entrepreneurs and businesses with investors, partners and funding opportunities.

## Components
- Investor membership
- Pitch days
- Investor lounge
- Project marketplace
- Business plan submissions
- Due diligence support
- Investor networking events

## Business Submissions
Businesses may submit:
- Executive summary
- Funding amount required
- Use of funds
- Industry
- Business stage
- Financial summary
- Contact details

## Investor Profiles
Investors can indicate:
- Preferred sectors
- Ticket size
- Countries of interest
- Risk appetite
- Partnership preference

## Pitch Events
Quarterly pitch events can be hosted for selected businesses.
