# FHBF Kingdom Enterprise Awards Framework

## Purpose
To celebrate excellence, innovation, integrity and impact among FHBF members and partners.

## Award Categories
- Entrepreneur of the Year
- SME of the Year
- Startup of the Year
- Young Entrepreneur of the Year
- Woman in Business Award
- Professional Excellence Award
- Business Innovation Award
- Community Impact Award
- Kingdom Enterprise Award
- Lifetime Achievement Award
- Sponsor of the Year
- Connector of the Year

## Nomination Process
1. Nominations open
2. Members submit nominations
3. Admin verifies eligibility
4. Judging panel reviews
5. Finalists announced
6. Winners awarded at gala/event

## Judging Criteria
- Integrity
- Business growth
- Innovation
- Community impact
- Leadership
- Sustainability
- Kingdom values
- Customer service
