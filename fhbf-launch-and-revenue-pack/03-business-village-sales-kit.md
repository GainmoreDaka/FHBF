# FHBF Business Village Sales Kit

## Why Exhibit?
Faith Heights Business Village gives businesses a direct opportunity to:
- Meet potential customers
- Showcase products and services
- Build partnerships
- Increase visibility
- Network with entrepreneurs and professionals
- Connect with sponsors and investors
- Participate in a premium Kingdom business environment

## Visitor Profile
Visitors may include:
- Conference delegates
- Business owners
- Church leaders
- Professionals
- Families
- Investors
- Students
- Corporate representatives
- Community leaders

## Exhibition Areas
- Kingdom Enterprise Pavilion
- SME Zone
- Startup Zone
- Professional Services Zone
- Property Zone
- Technology Zone
- Vehicle & Equipment Zone
- Investor Lounge
- Hospitality Area

## Booking Process
1. Choose space type
2. Confirm member or non-member status
3. Submit exhibitor details
4. Submit payment or proof of payment
5. Receive approval
6. Receive stand allocation
7. Attend setup briefing
8. Exhibit at Business Village

## Exhibitor Requirements
- Business name
- Contact person
- Phone/WhatsApp
- Email
- Product/service description
- Logo
- Electricity requirement
- Table/chair requirement
- Payment proof
