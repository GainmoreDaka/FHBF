# FHBF Trade Mission Framework

## Purpose
To expose members to new markets, suppliers, investors and international business opportunities.

## Possible Destinations
- South Africa
- Zambia
- Botswana
- Namibia
- Dubai
- Turkey
- China
- United Kingdom

## Mission Types
- Supplier sourcing
- Investment exploration
- Property and construction
- Agriculture
- Manufacturing
- Technology
- Import/export
- Franchise exploration

## Standard Mission Components
- Pre-mission briefing
- Business meetings
- Site visits
- Networking sessions
- Market briefing
- Group travel support
- Post-mission follow-up

## Member Benefits
- Discounted participation
- Group coordination
- Business introductions
- Supplier access
- Market exposure
- Partnership opportunities
