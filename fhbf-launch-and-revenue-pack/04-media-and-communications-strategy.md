# FHBF Media & Communications Strategy

## Purpose
To promote FHBF, increase membership, showcase member businesses, attract sponsors and communicate the Kingdom Enterprise Vision.

## Key Channels
- Website
- WhatsApp
- Facebook
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Email newsletter
- Church announcements
- Business forums

## Weekly Content
- Business Spotlight
- Member testimonial
- Business tip
- Event reminder
- Sponsor feature

## Monthly Content
- Member success story
- Leadership article
- Upcoming events digest
- Trade mission update
- Business Village update

## Quarterly Content
- Digital magazine
- Sponsor report
- Member impact report
- Opportunities report

## Content Themes
- Kingdom business
- Entrepreneurship
- Leadership
- Finance
- Trade
- Investment
- Innovation
- Testimonies
- Member stories

Monday: Business tip
