# FHBF Sponsorship Sales Kit

## Sponsorship Overview
Faith Heights Business Fellowship provides sponsors with access to entrepreneurs, professionals, business owners, investors, institutions and conference delegates.

Sponsors gain visibility, relationship-building opportunities and long-term partnership value.

- Website listing

- Social media acknowledgement
- Directory mention

### Silver Partner – US$1,000+
Benefits:
- Exhibition space
- Website feature

### Gold Partner – US$2,500+
Benefits:
- Premium Business Village placement

### Platinum Partner – US$5,000+
Benefits:
