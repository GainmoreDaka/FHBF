# FHBF Chapter Expansion Framework

## Purpose
To help FHBF expand into local, provincial and international chapters while maintaining consistency.

## Possible Chapters
- Harare
- Bulawayo
- Mutare
- Gweru
- Masvingo
- South Africa
- Zambia
- Botswana
- Namibia
- United Kingdom
- Europe
- USA

## Chapter Requirements
Each chapter should have:
- Chapter Coordinator
- Membership Lead
- Events Lead
- Prayer/Community Lead
- Communications Lead

## Chapter Activities
- Networking meetings
- Business breakfasts
- Prayer meetings
- Business forums
- Local directory growth
- Sponsor engagement
- Member support
- Trade and opportunity sharing

## Chapter Starter Pack
- FHBF Vision
- Membership guide
- Branding guide
- Event format guide
- Membership application links
- Reporting template
- Code of conduct
