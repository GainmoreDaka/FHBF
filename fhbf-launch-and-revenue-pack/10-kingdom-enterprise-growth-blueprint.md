# FHBF Kingdom Enterprise Growth Blueprint

## Purpose
To provide a non-political, Kingdom-centred long-term growth strategy.

## Growth Path

### Stage 1 – Foundation
- 100 Founding Members
- Business Village launch
- Basic directory
- Sponsor recruitment

### Stage 2 – Establishment
- 500 members
- 100 businesses listed
- Monthly events
- Opportunities hub
- Member spotlights

### Stage 3 – Expansion
- 1,000+ members
- Provincial chapters
- Trade missions
- Investor Connect
- Academy pilot

### Stage 4 – Ecosystem
- 2,500+ members
- Marketplace
- Mobile app
- Procurement network
- Annual awards

### Stage 5 – Regional Influence
- 5,000+ members
- International chapters
- Multiple trade missions
- Investor network
- FHBF media platform

## Strategic Targets
- Members: 100 → 500 → 1,000 → 2,500 → 5,000+
- Businesses listed: 50 → 250 → 750 → 1,500+
- Sponsors: 10 → 25 → 50+
- Business Village exhibitors: 50 → 100 → 250+
- Trade missions: 1 → 3 → 5+ annually

## Key Principle
FHBF should remain Kingdom-centred, business-focused, non-political and inclusive of all ethical businesses aligned with its values.
