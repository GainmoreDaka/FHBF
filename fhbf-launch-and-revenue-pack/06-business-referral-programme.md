# FHBF Business Referral Programme

## Purpose
To encourage members to actively support one another through referrals, introductions and business opportunities.

## Referral Types
- Customer referrals
- Supplier referrals
- Investor introductions
- Professional recommendations
- Partnership introductions
- New member referrals

## Recognition
Members can be recognised for:
- Referrals given
- Referrals received
- New members introduced
- Business generated
- Partnerships facilitated

## Annual Awards
- Connector of the Year
- Referral Champion
- Partnership Builder
- Business Growth Award
- Kingdom Collaboration Award

## Referral Principles
- Be honest
- Refer only where there is genuine fit
- Do not misrepresent
- Follow up professionally
- Honour confidentiality
- Give feedback where appropriate
