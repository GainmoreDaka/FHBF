# FHBF CRM Structure

## Purpose

The FHBF CRM should manage relationships, not just store names.

It should track:
- Members
- Prospective members
- Sponsors
- Exhibitors
- Visitors
- Investors
- Trade mission prospects
- Volunteers
- Partners

## CRM Groups

### Prospects
People interested in FHBF but not yet members.

### Applicants
People who have submitted a membership application.

### Active Members
Approved and paid members.

### Lapsed Members
Members whose membership has expired.

### Sponsors
Organisations supporting FHBF financially or strategically.

### Exhibitors
Businesses participating in Business Village.

### Partners
Institutions, churches, universities, corporates and organisations.

### Investors
Individuals or organisations interested in investment opportunities.

## CRM Goal

Every relationship should have:
- Clear status
- Contact person
- Next action
- Responsible team member
- Follow-up date
- Notes
