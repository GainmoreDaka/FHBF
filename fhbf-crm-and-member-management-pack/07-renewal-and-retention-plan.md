# FHBF Renewal & Retention Plan

## Renewal Timeline

### 60 Days Before Expiry
Send early reminder.

### 30 Days Before Expiry
Send renewal invitation.

### 14 Days Before Expiry
Send WhatsApp reminder.

### 7 Days Before Expiry
Personal follow-up.

### Expiry Day
Notify member of expiry.

### 14 Days After Expiry
Move to lapsed member status if unpaid.

## Retention Actions

- Ask why they have not renewed.
- Offer help with using benefits.
- Invite to next event.
- Remind them of directory and Business Village discounts.
- Offer upgrade/downgrade options.

## Retention Metrics

Track:
- Renewal rate
- Lapsed members
- At-risk members
- Most engaged members
- Least engaged members
