# FHBF Member Lifecycle Workflow

## Stage 1 – Interest

Person hears about FHBF through:
- Church announcement
- WhatsApp
- Website
- Business Village
- Referral
- Social media
- Networking event

Status:
Prospect

## Stage 2 – Application

Prospect completes online application.

Status:
Applicant

## Stage 3 – Review

Membership team checks:
- Contact details
- Membership type
- Business information
- Payment proof
- Directory consent

Status:
Under Review

## Stage 4 – Approval

Admin approves application.

System generates:
- Membership number
- Digital card
- Directory profile
- Welcome message

Status:
Active Member

## Stage 5 – Engagement

Member participates in:
- Events
- Directory
- Business Village
- Marketplace
- Opportunities
- Trade missions

Status:
Engaged Member

## Stage 6 – Renewal

System reminds member before expiry.

Status:
Renewal Due

## Stage 7 – Retention

If member does not renew:
- Follow-up call
- WhatsApp reminder
- Offer support
- Ask for feedback

Status:
At Risk / Lapsed
