# FHBF Exhibitor CRM Workflow

## Exhibitor Stages

### Interested
Business asks for information.

### Applied
Registration form submitted.

### Awaiting Payment
Booking received but payment not confirmed.

### Approved
Business approved to exhibit.

### Stand Allocated
Stand assigned.

### Setup Ready
Exhibitor has received setup information.

### Checked In
Exhibitor arrived and set up.

### Followed Up
Post-event follow-up completed.

## Exhibitor Data

- Business name
- Contact person
- Phone / WhatsApp
- Email
- Product/service category
- Stand type
- Stand number
- Electricity required
- Table required
- Payment status
- Setup notes
- Special requirements
