# FHBF Member Onboarding Journey

## Day 0 – Application Approved

Send:
- Welcome WhatsApp
- Welcome email
- Membership number
- Digital card
- Instructions to complete profile

## Day 1 – Profile Setup

Ask member to upload:
- Business logo
- Business description
- Services
- WhatsApp number
- Website
- Photos

## Day 3 – Directory Activation

Notify member:
Your FHBF directory listing is now live.

## Day 7 – Engagement

Invite member to:
- Join WhatsApp group
- View upcoming events
- Register for Business Village
- Submit opportunities
- Follow FHBF social media

## Day 14 – Support

Ask:
- Do you need help with your profile?
- Are you interested in exhibiting?
- Would you like mentorship or introductions?

## Day 30 – Retention

Send:
- Member benefits reminder
- Upcoming events
- Business spotlight opportunity
