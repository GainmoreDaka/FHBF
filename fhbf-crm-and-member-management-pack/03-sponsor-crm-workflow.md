# FHBF Sponsor CRM Workflow

## Sponsor Stages

### Lead
Potential sponsor identified.

### Contacted
Initial message or proposal sent.

### Meeting Booked
Sponsor meeting scheduled.

### Proposal Sent
Sponsor prospectus shared.

### Negotiation
Discussing package and benefits.

### Confirmed
Sponsor agrees.

### Paid
Payment received.

### Activated
Sponsor benefits delivered.

### Renewal
Sponsor invited to renew.

## Sponsor Data To Track

- Organisation name
- Contact person
- Email
- Phone
- Industry
- Sponsorship level
- Amount
- Benefits promised
- Benefits delivered
- Payment status
- Renewal date
- Notes

## Follow-Up Rule

Every sponsor lead should have a next follow-up date.
