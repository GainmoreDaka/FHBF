# FHBF Lead & Follow-Up System

## Lead Sources

- Website form
- WhatsApp enquiry
- Business Village
- Referral
- Event attendance
- Social media
- Sponsor introduction

## Lead Types

- Membership lead
- Sponsor lead
- Exhibitor lead
- Investor lead
- Trade mission lead
- Marketplace lead
- Academy lead

## Follow-Up Fields

- Lead name
- Organisation
- Contact details
- Lead type
- Source
- Status
- Assigned to
- Next action
- Follow-up date
- Notes

## Statuses

- New
- Contacted
- Interested
- Application Sent
- Application Received
- Payment Pending
- Completed
- Not Interested
- Follow Up Later

## Follow-Up Principle

No lead should remain without a next action.
