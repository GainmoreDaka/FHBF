# FHBF Founding Member Prospectus

## Faith Heights Business Fellowship

### Building Businesses. Developing Leaders. Transforming Nations.

Faith Heights Business Fellowship is launching a year-round Kingdom business ecosystem for entrepreneurs, professionals, investors, institutions and leaders.

## Founding Member Opportunity

The Founding Member Programme recognises the first members who help establish FHBF as a permanent business platform.

## Who Can Join

- Entrepreneurs
- Professionals
- Business owners
- Investors
- Corporate leaders
- Students and youth entrepreneurs
- Ministries and institutions
- SMEs and established companies

## Founding Member Benefits

- Founding Member Certificate
- Founding Member Badge on website
- Priority Business Village booking
- Directory listing
- Recognition on Founders Wall
- Invitation to Founders Reception
- Early access to events and trade missions
- Member-only networking opportunities
- Discounted Business Village rates

## Why Join Now

FHBF is not only about one event. It is being built as a long-term Kingdom business network offering:
- Visibility
- Customers
- Partnerships
- Mentorship
- Trade missions
- Events
- Marketplace opportunities
- Investment connections

## Founding Member Call to Action

Become part of the foundation of a Kingdom business movement that will support enterprise, leadership and community transformation for years to come.
