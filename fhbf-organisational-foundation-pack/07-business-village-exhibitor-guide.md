# Faith Heights Business Village Exhibitor Guide

## Event Overview

Faith Heights Business Village is the flagship marketplace and networking hub of Faith Heights Business Fellowship.

It provides a platform for businesses, professionals, investors, sponsors and organisations to showcase products, services, ideas and opportunities.

## Venue

Cathedral of Faith, Belvedere, Harare.

## Site Concept

- Main entrance facing the Cathedral.
- Controlled access with barriers.
- Large central marquee in the main blue zone.
- Smaller marquees and displays around the perimeter.
- Vehicle and equipment displays along accessible outer areas.
- Trees and bushes preserved where possible.
- One controlled hospitality/vendor approach.

## Main Zones

### Kingdom Enterprise Pavilion
Main marquee for premium exhibitors, corporates, sponsors, banks, universities and major organisations.

### SME Zone
For small and medium businesses.

### Startup Zone
For youth entrepreneurs and emerging businesses.

### Professional Services Zone
For lawyers, accountants, consultants, healthcare and advisory firms.

### Property Zone
For developers, estate agents, construction and real estate businesses.

### Technology Zone
For software, AI, telecoms, renewable energy and digital services.

### Vehicle & Equipment Zone
For car dealers, machinery, agricultural equipment and construction equipment.

### Investor Lounge
Premium area for investor meetings, sponsor networking and private discussions.

### Hospitality Area
Controlled vendor model only.

## Exhibitor Rules

- Exhibitors must register and be approved.
- Full payment must be made before setup.
- No counterfeit, illegal or inappropriate products.
- No unauthorised food vendors.
- Displays must remain within allocated space.
- Exhibitors must keep stands clean and staffed.
- Vehicle movement must be restricted during public opening hours.
- Electrical requests must be submitted in advance.
- FHBF reserves the right to relocate stands for safety or operational reasons.

## Setup Requirements

Exhibitors should provide:
- Business name
- Contact person
- Product/service description
- Electricity requirements
- Table/chair requirements
- Logo
- Payment proof
- Any special display needs

## Visitor Flow

Visitors enter through the Cathedral-facing main entrance, pass registration/check-in, proceed through the Seven Mountains entrance experience, visit the main marquee, then flow to outer displays, vehicle areas, networking and hospitality zones.
