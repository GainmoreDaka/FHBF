# FHBF Membership Handbook

## Welcome

Welcome to Faith Heights Business Fellowship. You are joining a year-round Kingdom business ecosystem designed to help members connect, grow, trade, invest, learn and influence society through ethical enterprise.

## Membership Levels

### Student Member
For students, young entrepreneurs and startups.

### Entrepreneur Member
For sole traders, SMEs, freelancers and emerging business owners.

### Professional Member
For professionals such as accountants, lawyers, doctors, consultants, engineers and executives.

### Business Member
For registered businesses and growing companies.

### Corporate Partner
For institutions, corporates, banks, universities, insurers, telecoms, vehicle companies and major partners.

### Founding Member
Special recognition for the first approved founding members.

## Member Benefits

Members may receive:
- FHBF membership number.
- Digital membership card.
- Directory listing.
- Business profile page.
- Business Village discounts.
- Event access.
- Networking opportunities.
- Member-only updates.
- Trade mission opportunities.
- Marketplace access.
- Opportunities board access.
- Prayer and encouragement network.
- Training and mentorship opportunities.

## Member Responsibilities

Members are expected to:
- Conduct business with integrity.
- Honour commitments and payments.
- Respect other members.
- Avoid false claims or misleading advertising.
- Keep directory information accurate.
- Use FHBF platforms responsibly.
- Promote the reputation of FHBF positively.

## Business Directory Rules

Listings must:
- Be accurate and truthful.
- Use appropriate images and wording.
- Avoid offensive or illegal content.
- Be approved by FHBF admin before publication.
- Be updated when details change.

## Networking Etiquette

Members should:
- Be respectful and professional.
- Avoid aggressive selling.
- Follow up responsibly.
- Honour appointments.
- Share opportunities fairly.
- Give referrals honestly.

## Code of Conduct

FHBF promotes:
- Integrity
- Excellence
- Respect
- Accountability
- Service
- Fair dealing
- Kingdom values

FHBF may remove members who use the platform for fraud, manipulation, harassment, exploitation or conduct that damages the fellowship.

## Renewal

Membership is renewable annually unless otherwise approved. Members should renew before expiry to maintain directory listing, benefits and discounts.
