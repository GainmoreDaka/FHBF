# FHBF Sponsor & Partnership Prospectus

## Overview

Faith Heights Business Fellowship offers sponsors and partners access to a growing network of entrepreneurs, professionals, business owners, investors, institutions and conference delegates.

## Partnership Opportunities

### Bronze Partner
Includes:
- Website listing
- Event recognition
- Directory mention
- Social media acknowledgement

### Silver Partner
Includes:
- Exhibition space
- Website feature
- Event signage
- Social media promotion
- Directory placement

### Gold Partner
Includes:
- Premium Business Village placement
- Main programme recognition
- Website homepage feature
- Sponsor profile page
- Media mentions
- VIP networking access

### Platinum Partner
Includes:
- Major branding
- Prime exhibition position
- Speaking opportunity consideration
- Investor Lounge access
- Year-round recognition
- Corporate directory feature

### Kingdom Enterprise Partner
Highest level strategic partner.
Includes:
- Signature branding opportunities
- Strategic partnership recognition
- Premium visibility across FHBF platforms
- Business Village headline positioning
- Year-round partnership benefits

Sponsors gain:
- Brand visibility
