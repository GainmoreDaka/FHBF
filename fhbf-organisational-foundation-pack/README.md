# FHBF Organisational Foundation Pack

This pack gives Faith Heights Business Fellowship the organisational structure needed to operate as a serious Kingdom business network.

Included documents:
1. FHBF Constitution Draft
2. Membership Handbook
3. Governance & Operations Manual
4. Founding Member Prospectus
5. Sponsor & Partnership Prospectus
6. Business Village Exhibitor Guide
7. Kingdom Enterprise Vision Statement

These are working drafts and should be reviewed by FHBF leadership and, where necessary, by a legal/governance adviser before formal adoption.
