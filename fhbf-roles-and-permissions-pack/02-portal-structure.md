# FHBF Portal Structure

## Public Website
Home, About, Kingdom Enterprise Vision, Membership, Directory, Business Village, Events, Trade Missions, Sponsors and Contact.

## Member Portal
Dashboard, My Membership, Digital Card, My Profile, My Business, My Events, My Bookings, Opportunities, Trade Missions, Referrals and Messages.

## Admin Portal
Dashboard, Membership Applications, Members, Businesses, Business Village, Events, Sponsors, Payments, CRM, Reports and Settings.

## Exhibitor Portal
My Stand, Setup Instructions, Payment Status, Exhibitor Documents, Event Notices and Lead Capture.

## Sponsor Portal
Sponsor Dashboard, Package Benefits, Branding Uploads, Invoices, Event Invitations and Reports.

## Chapter Portal
Chapter Dashboard, Local Members, Chapter Events, Reports, Local Sponsors and Chapter Announcements.
