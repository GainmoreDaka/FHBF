# Sponsor, Exhibitor and Chapter Portals

## Sponsor Portal
Shows sponsor package, benefits included, benefits delivered, invoices, events and branding status.

Actions:
- Upload logo
- Upload advert
- Download invoice
- Confirm attendance
- Request meeting

## Exhibitor Portal
Shows event name, stand number, zone, setup time, payment status and required documents.

Actions:
- Upload logo
- Upload payment proof
- Download setup guide
- View rules
- Submit special requirements

## Chapter Portal
Shows chapter members, new applications, local events, local sponsors, follow-ups and reports due.

Actions:
- Submit chapter report
- Add local event
- View local member list
- Export chapter report
