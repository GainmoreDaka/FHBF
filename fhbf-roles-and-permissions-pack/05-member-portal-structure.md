# FHBF Member Portal Structure

## Dashboard
Shows membership status, membership number, renewal date, profile completion, events, bookings, directory views, opportunities and notifications.

## My Membership
Membership tier, start date, expiry date, payment status, renewal button and upgrade option.

## Digital Membership Card
Member photo, name, membership number, QR code, membership level and expiry date.

## My Business
Business logo, cover image, description, services, products, gallery, WhatsApp, website and social links.

## My Events
Registered events, tickets, QR codes and attendance history.

## My Business Village
Stand bookings, stand numbers, payment status and setup instructions.

## Opportunities
View, save, apply or enquire.

## Referrals
Referral code, referrals made, referrals received and rewards.
