# FHBF Admin Dashboard Structure

## Super Admin
Can manage all areas.

Dashboard cards:
- Active Members
- Pending Applications
- Membership Revenue
- Business Village Revenue
- Sponsors
- Pending Payments
- Directory Businesses
- Follow-Ups Due

## Membership Admin
Applications, approvals, rejections, renewals, membership numbers, cards and member messages.

## Business Village Admin
Village layout, zones, stands, bookings, exhibitors, setup instructions and check-in.

## Events Admin
Events, tickets, registrations, QR check-in and attendance reports.

## Sponsor Admin
Sponsor leads, packages, benefits, logo uploads, invoices and renewals.

## Finance Admin
Payments, proof of payment, invoices, reports, outstanding balances and refunds.

## CRM Admin
Leads, follow-ups, prospect pipeline, sponsor pipeline, exhibitor pipeline and member engagement.

## Chapter Admin
Chapter members, chapter events, local sponsors and reports.
