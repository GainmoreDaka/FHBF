# FHBF User Role Overview

## Public Visitor
Can view website, public directory, events, sponsors, membership page and Business Village information.

## Applicant
Can view application status, update contact details, upload proof of payment and receive admin messages.

## Member
Can access member dashboard, digital membership card, events, opportunities, trade missions and Business Village bookings.

## Business Member
Can manage business profile, services, products, enquiries, directory analytics and Business Village bookings.

## Corporate Partner
Can manage corporate profile, team users, sponsor opportunities, tenders/RFQs and corporate benefits.

## Exhibitor
Can view stand booking, setup instructions, payment status, documents and exhibitor notices.

## Sponsor
Can view sponsor package, upload logo/materials, view benefits, download invoices and access sponsor reports.

## Mentor
Can view mentee requests, manage availability, upload resources and track mentoring sessions.

## Chapter Leader
Can manage local members, chapter events, sponsor leads and chapter reports.

## Advisory Council
Can view strategic reports, advisory documents and submit recommendations.

## Admin Roles
Super Admin, Membership Admin, Business Village Admin, Events Admin, Sponsor Admin, Finance Admin, CRM Admin, Academy Admin and Chapter Admin.
