# FHBF Gamma Technical Blueprint

Faith Heights Business Fellowship (FHBF) is designed as a year-round Kingdom business ecosystem, not only an event website. The Business Village is the flagship event module, while membership, directory, marketplace, opportunities, trade missions, and investor connect operate throughout the year.

## Core Modules
1. Membership CRM
2. Business Directory
3. Business Village Booking
4. Events & QR Check-In
5. Opportunities Hub
6. Marketplace
7. Trade Missions
8. Investor Connect
9. Sponsor Portal
10. Admin Dashboard
11. Notifications: Email, WhatsApp, SMS
12. Campus Portal / Free Wi-Fi Access Pages

## Recommended Stack
- Frontend: Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
- Backend: NestJS or Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- ORM: Prisma or Drizzle
- Auth: Auth.js, Clerk, or custom JWT
- Storage: Cloudflare R2 / AWS S3
- Payments: Stripe, PayPal, EcoCash, ZIPIT, bank transfer proof upload
- Notifications: WhatsApp Business API, Email SMTP, SMS gateway
- Deployment: Docker, PostgreSQL, Nginx, VPS/cloud server
