Vision

A Kingdom Business Ecosystem, Not Just One Event

FHBF hosts networking events, expos, trade missions, business tours, mentorship sessions, prayer meetings, marketplace opportunities and the annual Business Village. The Village is the current flagship event, but the fellowship operates all year.

Our purpose is to create meaningful connections, foster collaboration, unlock opportunities and empower members to grow sustainable businesses while advancing Kingdom values.
