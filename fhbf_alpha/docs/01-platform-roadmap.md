# FHBF Platform Roadmap

## Positioning
Faith Heights Business Fellowship is a year-round Kingdom business network. The Business Village is the current flagship event, but the platform must support many events, expos, business tours, trade missions, training sessions, investor meetings and networking activities.

## Alpha Release
Public website, membership application, business directory, Business Village registration, member pricing, admin preview, opportunities hub and events/trade mission pages.

## Beta Release
Real authentication, database persistence, admin approvals, QR membership card generation, event check-in, WhatsApp notifications and member dashboard.

## Production Release
Payments, sponsor portal, investor connect, marketplace transactions, referrals, mentor matching, analytics and mobile app API.

## Long-Term Vision
Build FHBF into a Kingdom Chamber of Commerce / business ecosystem for Zimbabwe, Africa and the diaspora.
