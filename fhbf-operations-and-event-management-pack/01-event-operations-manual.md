# FHBF Event Operations Manual

## Purpose
To provide a repeatable operating system for all FHBF events.

## Event Types
- Networking Breakfasts
- Kingdom Enterprise Roundtables
- Investor Connect Forums
- Trade & Export Forums
- Business Village
- Awards Gala
- Sponsor Receptions
- Prayer Breakfasts
- Trade Mission Briefings

## Planning Timeline

### 12 Weeks Before
- Confirm event purpose
- Confirm venue
- Prepare budget
- Appoint event lead
- Confirm target audience
- Prepare sponsorship plan

### 8 Weeks Before
- Open registration
- Confirm speakers
- Start marketing
- Confirm vendors
- Prepare volunteer plan

### 4 Weeks Before
- Confirm guest list
- Confirm signage
- Confirm media plan
- Confirm programme
- Confirm payments

### 1 Week Before
- Final briefing
- Print materials
- Confirm volunteers
- Send reminders
- Test registration/check-in

### Event Day
- Setup
- Registration
- Hospitality
- Programme delivery
- Photography/media
- Issue resolution
- Breakdown

### Post Event
- Send thanks
- Collect feedback
- Produce report
- Follow up leads
- Update CRM
