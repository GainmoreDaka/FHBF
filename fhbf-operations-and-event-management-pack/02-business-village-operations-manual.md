# FHBF Business Village Operations Manual

## Site Principles
- Main entrance faces the Cathedral.
- Other sides controlled with barriers.
- Main marquee in the central blue area.
- Smaller marquees and displays around the main area.
- Vehicle displays along accessible outer edges.
- Trees and bushes preserved.
- Food vendors restricted to approved suppliers only.

## Pre-Event Tasks
- Confirm site map
- Confirm tent/marquee supplier
- Confirm power and lighting
- Allocate stands
- Confirm exhibitor payments
- Issue exhibitor handbook
- Confirm signage
- Confirm security and volunteer teams
- Confirm official hospitality vendors

## Event Day Teams
- Main entrance team
- QR/check-in team
- Exhibitor support team
- Sponsor/VIP team
- Vehicle movement team
- Hospitality liaison
- Security liaison
- First aid liaison
- Media team

## Event Day Rules
- No vehicle movement during public opening hours unless authorised.
- No unauthorised food vendors.
- Walkways must remain clear.
- Exhibitors must remain within allocated spaces.
- Emergency routes must remain open.
- Setup and breakdown times must be enforced.

## Post Event
- Confirm site cleared
- Collect exhibitor feedback
- Collect sponsor feedback
- Produce event report
- Update CRM
- Invite exhibitors to become members
