# FHBF Health & Safety Pack

## Risk Areas
- Crowd movement
- Vehicle displays
- Electrical cabling
- Weather
- Slips/trips
- Food hygiene
- Fire risk
- Medical emergencies
- Children near displays
- Equipment displays

## Basic Controls
- Keep walkways clear
- Mark emergency routes
- Restrict vehicle movement
- Tape down cables
- Provide first aid point
- Use approved vendors
- Keep fire points visible
- Use barriers where needed
- Brief volunteers

## Incident Form Fields
- Date/time
- Location
- Person involved
- Description
- Witnesses
- Action taken
- Reported to
- Follow-up required

## Emergency Plan
- Identify first aid point
- Identify nearest emergency exit
- Identify responsible security lead
- Identify vehicle access route
- Keep emergency route clear
- Document incidents
