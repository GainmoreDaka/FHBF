# FHBF Trade Mission Operations Guide

## Purpose
To expose members to markets, suppliers, investors and business opportunities.

## Destinations
- South Africa
- Zambia
- Botswana
- Namibia
- Dubai
- Turkey
- China
- United Kingdom

## Planning Steps
- Choose destination and focus
- Identify partners
- Confirm dates
- Prepare itinerary
- Estimate cost
- Open applications
- Collect deposits
- Hold briefing
- Conduct mission
- Follow up opportunities

## Mission Components
- Business meetings
- Site visits
- Market briefing
- Networking event
- Supplier visits
- Investment meetings
- Post-mission report

## Participant Documents
- Passport copy
- Visa where required
- Emergency contact
- Business profile
- Payment record
