# FHBF Kingdom Enterprise Awards Gala Guide

## Purpose
To recognise excellence, integrity, innovation and impact.

## Award Categories
- Entrepreneur of the Year
- SME of the Year
- Startup of the Year
- Young Entrepreneur
- Woman in Business
- Professional Excellence
- Innovation Award
- Community Impact Award
- Kingdom Enterprise Award
- Lifetime Achievement Award

## Workflow
1. Open nominations
2. Verify nominees
3. Shortlist finalists
4. Judging panel reviews
5. Announce finalists
6. Prepare certificates/trophies
7. Host awards
8. Publish winners
9. Feature winners online

## Event Elements
- Red carpet/photos
- Sponsor tables
- Dinner or refreshments
- Programme booklet
- Certificates
- Media coverage
