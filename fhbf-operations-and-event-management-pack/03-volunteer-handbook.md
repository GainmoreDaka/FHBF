# FHBF Volunteer Handbook

## Volunteer Values
- Excellence
- Service
- Respect
- Professionalism
- Kingdom hospitality

## Volunteer Teams

### Registration Team
Handles visitor welcome, QR scanning, badges and information.

### Exhibitor Support Team
Helps exhibitors find stands, resolve issues and follow setup instructions.

### Parking & Vehicle Team
Controls loading, unloading, parking and vehicle displays.

### Hospitality Team
Supports official hospitality vendor, VIP refreshments and guest care.

### VIP & Sponsor Team
Welcomes sponsors, advisory leaders, speakers and special guests.

### Media Team
Captures photos, videos, interviews and testimonials.

### Prayer Team
Provides prayer support and encouragement.

### Security Liaison Team
Works with security personnel and helps manage controlled access.

## Volunteer Conduct
- Arrive on time
- Wear identification
- Follow instructions
- Be polite and calm
- Escalate problems quickly
- Do not make unauthorised promises
